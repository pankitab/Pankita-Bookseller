Health Program

Our Gut Health & Weight Loss Program is an easy to follow 3-step process that will focus on a lifestyle adjustment towards healthier living for the rest of your life. We provide nutritional & lifestyle coaching along with support to empower people to achieve their health goals.

How It Works

Phatt Program offers you an innovative 3-step program that will focus on a lifestyle adjustment towards healthier living for the rest of your life. Backed by the “Putting Health at the Top” team, this unique program is designed to help you detox, balance your gut health and in-turn promotes healthy weight loss. Our Program comes with Nutritionist approved meal plans supported by high quality products, Nutrition Guide , Dedicated one-on-one Mentorship at no-added cost, access to our private recipe pages and online community of more than 400,000 like-minded members sharing their inspiring weight loss stories along with top tips & tricks to help you along the way!

Reset

Reset

You begin your health journey by eating from a list of approved foods with no calorie counting for 3 days and starting your nutrition.

The focus of step 1 is to gently improve your gut health by removing sugar and refined carbohydrates.

Fat Loss & Detox

Fat Loss & Detox

This is the detox & weight loss step which typically lasts for 27 days. Your meals will be calorie restricted & supported by proper nutrition

This is when your body will start to change as a result of restricting calories and burning fat.

Sustain

Sustain

We re-introduce additional food groups into your diet along with calories increase to begin to teach you how to live healthily for the rest of your life.

This is where you teach your body to keep the weight off.

Rest Of The Life

Rest Of The Life

This is final stage where we suggest 80:20 lifestyle. At this point 80% of the time you implement what you learned on your Ph@tt journry and then 20% you eat what you wish to as now you know how to restore your gut health.

Daily

Throughout the day you must consume 3 liters of water

Morning

First thing in the morning, you take your nutrition with some water and protein

Morning Snack

For Morning snack, you have half a piece of fruit from approved food list

Lunch

For lunch, you have your nutrition, protein (veg or non-veg protein) and One vegetable from approved food list

Afternoon Snack

Another half piece of fruit (from approved food list) for an afternoon snack

Dinner

For dinner, you have more protein (not red meat this time) and ONE vegetable , plus additional nutrition
